Required Vercel env vars

  • CRON_SECRET — random string (cron auth)
  • NAUKRI_COOKIES_JSON — cookies from naukri_cookies.json
  • BLOB_READ_WRITE_TOKEN — Vercel Blob (Excel history)
  • OPENROUTER_API_KEY — optional
